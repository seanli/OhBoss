A Platform For Interest Groups & Sponsors.

Fundstrap helps raise funding for interest groups by providing a platform which improves the process of matching sponsors/donors with desirable interest groups.

Matching

Fundstrap acts as an aggregator for all interest groups and sponsors with their detailed profiles, allowing them to get matched with desirable parties.

Workflow

Fundstrap provides a smooth workflow for fundraising, as well as features to conveniently and effectively manage and keep track of sponsorships, ensuring the synchronization of fundraising workflow between interest groups and sponsors.

Collaboration

Emcompassing crowdfunding capabilities, Fundstrap allows the collaboration between multiple interest groups and multiple sponsors to effectively gather enough funding for a single big event or cause.

Protection

Fundstrap protects sponsors with a rating and feedback system on the capability and credibility of listed interest groups. Sponsors can also apply for a percentage of “refund” through our platform, if the interest groups did not fulfil their initial expectations.

Privacy

Interest groups and sponsors can share their detailed information without worrying about leaking sensitive information, because Fundstrap offers high level security and different privacy options, so that only relevant and safe information will be shared among users.

Analytics

Fundstrap is able to record numerous metrics for interest groups and sponsors, so that they can use these crucial data to improve their organizations.

What Problems Are We Solving?


Interest groups often lack funding to bring higher quality experience to their members or to fulfill their cause.

There is not one online system where sponsorships for interest groups can be conveniently recorded and managed. Sometimes there can be inconsistencies between the sponsorship agreements between interest groups and their sponsors or even miscommunications leading to the loss of sponsorships.

There is not one place where interest groups and sponsors/donors can share their detailed information with the appropriate privacy options, which allows them to be effectively paired according to their preferences.

Cost of running public relations for sponsorships can be very high, especially since a lot of interest groups are non-profitable, and it may require a lot of time and energy for the groups’ members to attract sponsors.


Who Are Our Customers?


Interest groups, such as campus clubs, charitable organizations, sports teams who are looking for sponsorships. Restaurants, vendors, service providers, and other types of businesses who are willing to sponsor events or causes. Individuals or organizations who want to invest to make a difference on their community.


What is Our Mission?


Our mission is to present a professional and high quality platform which brings together interest groups and sponsors/donors. We aim to provide top-notch services for sponsorship matching, sponsorship management, crowdfunding, privacy, and protecting the benefits of all parties. Fundstrap will be the ONE hub for all interest groups and sponsors/donors.
